ABOUT US

The India Club at Lehigh University is a non-profit organization formed primarily by graduate students of Indian origin, whose purpose is to cherish the rich and vibrant culture of India. The club provides a platform to provide visibility of Indian culture to students at Lehigh and to display our creativity through participation and organization of cultural exhibitions which capture the celebrative mood of various Indian festivities.


The club is open to all and anybody can participate in the events. Most of our events are open to all students.
